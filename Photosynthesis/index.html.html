PHOTOSYNTHESIS

It is the process by which plants make their own food.
Therefore, plants are also called 'Autotrophs'.
Interested to know more
Okay... Let's continue.

First of all, we need to know about the raw materials of photosynthesis.
So what are they?

  1. Sunlight.
  2. Chlorophyll.
  3. Water.
  4. Carbon dioxide(CO2).

Did you know?
Chlorophyll is a green pigment which is already present in the plants.
Because of this pigment, the plants have green colour just like our blood has red colour due to the presence of HAEMOGLOBIN.

Let's start the process...
1. The CHLOROPHYLL present in the plants absorbs the SUNLIGHT.
2. The roots absorbs WATER from the soil.
3. The stomata absorbs CARBON DIOXIDE from the atmosphere.

Our raw materials are absorbed...
Now what happen?
1. The absorbed Sunlight will split the water(H2O) into Hydrogen& Oxygen.
2. Oxygen is then given out by the plants through stomata.
3. Hydrogen is joined in the CO2 gas and forms Glucose(C6H12O6).
4. And the Glucose is given to all parts of the plant.

This process gives out 2 output products.

  1. Glucose.
  2. Oxygen.

Did you know?
Photosynthesis is the process which occurs in daytime and not in night time.
We cannot sleep under a tree in the night, because in night time, the plants give out Carbon dioxide and we cannot respire it.
But in day time, it is good to sleep under the trees, because it gives Oxygen & Cooling Effect due to a process called Transpiration.
